GETTING STARTED

Create a new task, you want to do, and then click on 'SAVE' button to save the task.

DELETING TASK

If you want to remove any task, click on Delete button.

COMPLETED TASK

Click on 'COMPLETED' button when your task is completed

EDITING TASK

Edit your task by clicking on the 'Edit' button.

FILTERING TASK

You can see your Completed task, Incomplete task, and All task individually..
